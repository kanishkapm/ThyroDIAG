# Code of Conduct

## 1. Be Respectful
Treat others with respect and kindness. Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

## 2. Communicate Openly
Encourage a positive environment by communicating openly. Discussions should be constructive and aimed at fostering a healthy community.

## 3. Seek to Understand
Be open to understanding and considering other perspectives. Approach conflicts with a willingness to learn and grow.

## 4. Support Others
Support fellow community members, especially newcomers. Offer help and be patient with those who may need guidance.

## 5. Report Issues
If you encounter any issues or violations of these guidelines, report them to a designated leader or administrator.

## 6. Have Fun!
Remember to have fun and enjoy the process of collaboration! 

## **Enforcement**
Community leaders will enforce this code of conduct and may take appropriate actions for violations, including warning or expulsion from the community.

## **Thanks**
Thank you for being part of our community and helping us create a safe and enjoyable space for everyone!
